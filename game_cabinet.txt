Game cabinet is a service to control following devices connected to retropie
raspberry distribution.
LCD
2 [5V] black
6 [GND] white
3 [GPIO2-SDA] green
5 [GPIO3-SCL] yellow

Relay
4 [5V] black
9 [GND] white
7 [GPIO4] orange

LED PWM
17 [3v3] 
34 [GND]
33 [GPIO13-PWM1]
 

monitor: power on/off
audio amplifier: power on/off
leds under cabinet: pwm brightness control
leds at the top (behind bezel): on/off
lcd display: control of display
